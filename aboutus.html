    About Us

    Hello and Welcome to Shammy's Cuisine!...

    I’m Shammy, an engineer by degree and a former IT professional with 15 years of experience. After years of excelling in the corporate world, I chose to embrace my role as a homemaker and proud mom of two beautiful kids. Along the way, my passion for cooking and baking blossomed into something extraordinary, and now, it’s my mission to share that joy with others. Cooking has always been more than just a task for me; it’s an art and an expression of love. Over the years, I’ve developed a knack for crafting meals that are not only delicious but also healthy and nourishing. From hearty comfort foods to balanced, nutrient-rich dishes, my recipes are inspired by family traditions, global flavors, and my commitment to quality ingredients. As I embark on this exciting journey of starting my own kitchen in Paris, my goal is to bring the warmth of home-cooked meals to your table. Every dish is prepared with care, using fresh, high-quality ingredients, ensuring you get food that tastes just as good as it looks. Whether it’s a busy weekday dinner or a special occasion, I’m here to make your meals stress-free and delightful.
